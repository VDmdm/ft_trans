Game rules

Rules are short and very simple. For classic match you will have primitive pong game. For win you have to get 21 score!

How to create game?

Find games link on header, then go to new game. You can set password, background image, choose as many bonuses as you want to and e.t.c. Also you are free to create ranked match or classic.

How to join game?

Find games link on header. Here you can see game rooms, which you can join.

Guilds

You can join or create your own guild. Guilds can have wars between each other and have their own rank system.

For guild owners

You can set image to guild, choose officers and announce a war to other guilds.

War time

When the "War time" is active a member can ask to fight randomly anyone from the other guild, if no one of the other guild answer the call after a certain amount of time, the match is automatically won

Tournament

There are a permanent ladder tournament with a match making system with a ladder system. Users can play on the ladder, with a rating system, or play casually, without it affecting their rating. Rules can be different. You are allowed to register to the tournament in a timeframe before said tournament. After win you will get some prizes.
